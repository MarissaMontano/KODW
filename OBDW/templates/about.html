When you want recommendations, but don't want them THAT accurate


What is KODW?

It's an app that allows you to get music recommendations based off of your music prefrences, the more data you put in the more accurate the output. This is a recommendation system based solely off of what genres you like, and how you have previously rated the other songs that you have been given.


How does it work?

Based off of the genres you like and songs you rate, that data is fed into a classifier of your choice and then 10 new songs are selected for you based off of a pre-specified threshold
